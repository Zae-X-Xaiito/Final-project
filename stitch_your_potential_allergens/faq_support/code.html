Common Allergies

How do I identify seasonal allergy triggers? expand_more
Seasonal triggers are typically environmental. AllergyCare suggests monitoring the Allergy Map daily to correlate your symptom peaks with local pollen counts (trees, grass, or weeds). We recommend clinical testing if symptoms persist beyond typical peak windows.
Difference between intolerance and allergy? expand_more
An allergy involves an immune system response (IgE antibodies), whereas an intolerance is a digestive system issue. AllergyCare logs allow you to track the onset time of symptoms, which is a key clinical differentiator.

App Usage

How do I share my health records with my doctor? expand_more
Navigate to the Health Records tab in the side menu. Select 'Export Report' to generate a clinical-ready PDF of your symptom history and trigger logs, which can be emailed directly to your healthcare provider.

Account Security

Is my medical data encrypted? expand_more
Yes. AllergyCare utilizes 256-bit AES encryption for all data at rest and TLS for data in transit. Your Clinical User profile is protected by HIPAA-compliant infrastructure to ensure total patient privacy.
